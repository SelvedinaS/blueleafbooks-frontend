For Authors

Sell your digital books on BlueLeafBooks. Here’s how it works.

1. Create an author account

Register and choose Sell Books. You’ll get access to the Author Dashboard where you can upload and manage your books.

Register as author

2. Set your PayPal email

Earnings are sent directly to your PayPal account. You must add your PayPal email in Dashboard → Payout Settings before you can publish any book.

3. Upload your book

From the dashboard, go to Upload Book. You’ll need:

  • Title, description, genre, and price
  • Cover image (JPEG/PNG)
  • PDF file of the book

Once submitted, your book goes live and appears in the store.

4. Platform fee

BlueLeafBooks charges a platform fee on your sales. The exact percentage is shown in your dashboard and in the Terms & Conditions. You pay this fee manually (e.g. monthly) as explained in your dashboard. Unpaid fees can lead to your account being restricted until the balance is settled.

5. Payments

When a reader buys your book, payment is processed via PayPal. You receive your share (after the platform fee) according to the schedule and method described in your dashboard and in the Terms.

6. Rules and content

You are responsible for the content you upload. You must own the rights or have permission to sell the work. Plagiarism and infringing content are not allowed. BlueLeafBooks may remove content that violates these rules. See our Terms & Conditions for full details.
