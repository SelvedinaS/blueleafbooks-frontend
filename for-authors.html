How it works

1. Create your author account

Register, complete your profile, and add your PayPal email for verification, account records, and monthly payout processing.

2. Upload your PDF book

Add your cover, description, category, and price. Once published, your book becomes available for customers to purchase and download through the platform.

3. Get paid monthly

BlueLeafBooks receives customer payments, deducts the 5% platform fee, and processes author payouts on or around the 1st day of each month. Balances under $25 roll forward to the next payout cycle.

Important author terms

  • BlueLeafBooks processes customer payments through PayPal as the platform operator.
  • A fixed 5% platform fee is deducted from completed sales unless BlueLeafBooks states otherwise in writing.
  • Eligible author payouts are processed on or around the 1st day of each month for the previous month's cleared sales.
  • If the payable balance is below $25, the balance may be rolled over to the next monthly payout cycle.
  • BlueLeafBooks may delay or adjust payouts in cases of refunds, chargebacks, fraud review, policy violations, or missing payout information.
  • Authors remain responsible for the legality, ownership, and tax treatment of their content and earnings.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need PayPal before publishing?
Yes. You must add a valid PayPal email for verification and monthly payout processing before publishing.

When do I get paid?
BlueLeafBooks processes eligible author payouts on or around the 1st of each month for the previous month's cleared sales.

What happens if my balance is very small?
If your payable balance is below $25, it may be carried forward and included in the next monthly payout.

Do I keep ownership of my book?
Yes. You keep ownership of your content, subject to the rights needed for BlueLeafBooks to host, market, and deliver it through the platform.
