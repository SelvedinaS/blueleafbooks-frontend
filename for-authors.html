How it works

1. Create your author account

Register, complete your profile, and add your PayPal email for verification, account records, and settlement administration.

2. Upload your PDF book

Add your cover, description, category, and price. Once published, your book becomes available for customers to purchase and download through the platform.

3. Review monthly fee records

Your dashboard shows completed sales and the 5% platform fee calculated for the relevant calendar month.

Important author terms

  • BlueLeafBooks processes customer payments through PayPal as the platform operator.
  • A fixed 5% platform fee applies to completed sales unless BlueLeafBooks states otherwise in writing.
  • Authors must pay the monthly platform fee between the 1st and 10th day of the following month.
  • If fees are not paid on time, BlueLeafBooks may hide books, block uploads, or restrict the author account until the balance is settled.
  • Authors remain responsible for the legality, ownership, and tax treatment of their content and earnings.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need PayPal before publishing?
Yes. You must add a valid PayPal email for author verification and account administration before publishing.

When do I pay the platform fee?
You pay the total 5% platform fee for the previous calendar month between the 1st and 10th of the following month.

Do I keep ownership of my book?
Yes. You keep ownership of your content, subject to the rights needed for BlueLeafBooks to host, market, and deliver it through the platform.
