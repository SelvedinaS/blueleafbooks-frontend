How payments work

1. Customer buys

A reader purchases your digital book on BlueLeafBooks.

2. Sales are tracked automatically

Your completed sales are recorded in your dashboard, together with the 5% platform fee owed for the month.

3. Pay your monthly platform fee

You pay BlueLeafBooks the 5% platform fee between the 1st and 10th of the following month.

If a fee remains unpaid, your books may be temporarily hidden until the payment is marked as completed.
