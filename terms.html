Terms & Conditions

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

BlueLeafBooks is a digital marketplace where authors upload and sell PDF books, and customers can purchase and download them. By using the platform, you agree to these Terms.

2. Payments

Customers pay authors directly via PayPal. BlueLeafBooks does not hold customer funds for authors.

3. Platform Fee (Commission)

BlueLeafBooks charges a platform fee (commission) on each sale. The fee is calculated automatically and shown to authors in their dashboard.

4. Monthly Settlement & Deadline

At the end of each month, the platform calculates the total platform fee owed by the author for that month. Authors must pay the platform fee manually using the payment details provided by BlueLeafBooks.

Payment deadline: no later than the 10th day of the following month.

5. Late Payment & Restrictions

If the platform fee is not paid by the deadline, BlueLeafBooks may restrict the author account, including:

  • Blocking the author from uploading new books
  • Temporarily hiding the author’s books from the marketplace

Access may be restored after outstanding fees are settled.

6. Digital Products & Refunds

All products are digital downloads. Due to the nature of digital files, all sales are final once a download is available.

7. Author Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate PayPal details
  • Handle their own taxes and legal obligations
  • Pay platform fees on time
  • Upload only content they have rights to sell
