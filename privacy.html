Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BlueLeafBooks. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our digital book marketplace.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: For authors, we may collect additional information such as PayPal payout email address
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including books viewed, purchases made, and download activity
  • Payment Information: Payment-related information is processed securely through PayPal. We do not store full credit card details or sensitive payment data on our servers
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, authenticate your identity, and provide access to purchased content
  • Service Delivery: To process purchases, deliver digital books, and manage your library
  • Author Services: For authors, to track sales, calculate earnings, and process payouts to your configured PayPal email address
  • Communication: To send you important updates about your account, purchases, and platform notifications
  • Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and ensure platform security
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services

4. Payment Processing

All payment transactions are processed securely through PayPal. We do not store, process, or have access to your full credit card numbers, CVV codes, or other sensitive payment details. PayPal handles all payment data in accordance with their own privacy policy and security standards.

We only receive confirmation of successful payments and transaction IDs necessary to fulfill your orders.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login status and preferences
  • Maintain your shopping cart
  • Analyze how our platform is used to improve user experience
  • Provide basic analytics about platform usage

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though this may affect some functionality of the platform.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Account information is retained while your account is active. We may retain certain information for longer periods as required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention and financial record-keeping.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services, including PayPal for payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the platform after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Tax Information

BlueLeafBooks does not report, file, or manage taxes on behalf of authors. Authors are fully responsible for reporting their own income and paying all applicable taxes in accordance with the laws of their country of residence.

We may provide basic earnings summaries or reports for informational purposes only, but these do not constitute tax advice or official tax documents. Authors should consult their own tax advisors or local authorities regarding their tax obligations.
