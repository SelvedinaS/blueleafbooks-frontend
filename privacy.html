Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

BlueLeafBooks may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Account information (such as name and email address)
  • Login credentials (stored in encrypted form)
  • Purchase history and transaction records
  • Technical information (such as IP address, browser type, device data, and usage logs)

We collect only the information necessary to operate, maintain, and improve the platform.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

Personal data is processed for the purpose of providing services, fulfilling contractual obligations, maintaining platform security, and complying with applicable legal requirements.

3. Payments

All payments are processed securely through PayPal. BlueLeafBooks does not store, process, or have access to credit or debit card details.

Authors receive payments directly to their own PayPal accounts in accordance with the platform’s Terms & Conditions.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide access to purchased digital content
  • To process transactions and maintain records
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • To communicate important service-related information

5. Data Sharing

BlueLeafBooks does not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

We may share limited information with trusted service providers strictly for operational purposes, including payment processors (e.g., PayPal) and hosting or infrastructure providers. Such sharing is limited to what is necessary for platform functionality.

6. International Data Transfers

Some service providers (such as PayPal and hosting providers) may process data outside your country of residence. By using the platform, you acknowledge that such transfers may occur in accordance with applicable data protection standards.

7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data. Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

10. Children’s Privacy

BlueLeafBooks is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

11. Cookies

The platform may use essential cookies or similar technologies necessary for authentication, security, and functionality. We do not use cookies for unrelated tracking purposes.
