Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Digital Products

BlueLeafBooks sells digital books (PDF downloads) only. We do not sell physical goods.

2. General Policy

All sales are generally final once download access has been granted.

Due to the nature of digital content, we cannot revoke access to a file once it has been delivered or downloaded. For this reason, refunds are generally not provided after download access has been used, unless required by applicable law.

3. When a Refund May Be Considered

A refund may be considered only in limited circumstances, including:

  • Technical failure: You were unable to download the purchased file due to a confirmed platform or payment error, and the issue could not be resolved within a reasonable time.
  • Duplicate charge: You were charged more than once for the same purchase.
  • Wrong product: You received access to a different digital product than the one you purchased.

Any refund decision remains at BlueLeafBooks’ sole discretion unless otherwise required by law.

4. After Download

Once a book has been successfully downloaded or access to the file has been used, refunds are generally not available. Please review the product description carefully before completing your purchase.

5. How to Request a Refund or Report a Problem

If you believe your situation falls within one of the exceptions above, or if you experience a technical issue with your purchase, please contact us and include:

  • the email address used for the purchase
  • the approximate purchase date
  • the book title
  • a clear explanation of the issue

Contact email: blueleafbooks@hotmail.com

6. Payment Processor

Payments are processed through PayPal. Chargebacks and payment disputes may also be handled in accordance with PayPal’s own policies and procedures. We encourage customers to contact us first so we can attempt to resolve the issue directly.

7. Governing Law

This Refund Policy is governed by the laws of Bosnia and Herzegovina.

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact:
